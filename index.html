The Story

Healthcare providers are fed up. They tired of the patients who CHOOSE not to meet their daily vitamin requirements.

Rather than wait for these patient to become totally "un-recyclable," "The Provider" decided to turn these patients into fertilizer that can be use to nourish fruit trees that can go on to feed the hungry for years to come.

You are one of The Provider's patients.

Can you show The Provider that you can meet your daily vitamin requirement?

The Provider

"The Provider" is a healthcare provider. They could be a physician, a nurse practitioner, a specialist, or anyone licensed to provide care.

Instructions

Summary

This is a matching game.

Done in similar style to the game "Candy Crush." Credit to Ania Kubow for providing the tutorial for making a "Candy Crush" clone in JavaScript.

For details on how mine differs from Kubow's tutorial, please see README.md under this game's source code.

How To Play

Make matches of ROWS or COLUMNS to score.

Only matches of fruits and vegetables that are considered to be rich in the vitamin requested will count as part of the score.

See GUIDE to see which vitamins could be requested, and which fruits and vegetables goes with each vitamin.
